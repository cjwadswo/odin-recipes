Best Choclate Chip Cookies

Description

Crisp edges, chewy middles.

Ingridents

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons how water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet choclate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, choclate chips, and nuts, Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
